KampungKu

Date Created

9,08,2025

Purpose

Village information

Description

KampungKu is a mobile UI/UX design project created as a digital solution to improve community engagement and access to village-related information. Designed with a modern look and intuitive navigation, this app aims to bring the traditional concept of village communication into a more structured and accessible digital format. KampungKu is not only an information hub but also a platform that encourages participation, collaboration, and transparency among residents.

Objective

The primary objective of KampungKu is to provide a reliable and user-friendly prototype that supports effective communication within the community. By combining informative and interactive features, KampungKu seeks to simplify access to essential village data such as announcements, activity schedules, and organizational structures, while also providing channels for feedback and discussion. This project was specifically developed for a competition to showcase how digital innovation can positively impact social interaction and village administration.

Key features

-Village Information – Displays the organizational structure and key data about the village.
-Schedules – Provides reminders for patrol duties, community service, and other collective activities.
-Announcements – Keeps residents updated with the latest news and important notifications.
-Village Gallery – A place to showcase events, activities, and documentation of community life.
-Guest Reporting System – Allows residents to report visiting guests for better security and transparency.
-Suggestion Box – Enables residents to submit feedback and ideas for community development.
-Discussion Forum – A space for residents to communicate, ask questions, and share insights (requires login for interaction).

Tools

-Figma

Project outcome

The final outcome of KampungKu is a high-fidelity mobile application prototype that features clear interaction flows, a modern visual design, and functionalities tailored to the needs of village communities. This prototype demonstrates how technology can be utilized to strengthen community communication, enhance information transparency, and foster the creation of a safer, more organized, and participatory environment.
